BURJ KHALIFA

World's Tallest Building

The tallest building in the world, Burj Khalifa towers over Dubai at 2,722 feet. Considered to be an engineering feat as well as an architectural marvel, the building is home to a luxurious hotel, fine-dining restaurants, and observation decks that offer some of the best views of the Dubai skyline.

Rahul Mehta
